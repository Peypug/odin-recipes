Cinnamon Roll Dump Cake

About

A Cinnamon Roll Dump Cake is canned cinnamon rolls mixed with cream cheese frosting and coffe cake mix.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • Cans of cinnamon rolls: 2
  • Container of cream cheese frosting: 1
  • Chopped toasted pecans: 1/2 cups
  • Package of coffe cake mix: 1
  • Salted butter: 3/4 cups

Baking process

  1. Preheat 350 F, spray 9x13 baking pan, and apply parchment paper
  2. Put cinnamon rolls in pan
  3. Frost cinnamon rolls and sprinkle pecans
  4. Evenly spread cake mix over cinnamon rolls, arrange butter evenly over mix, and sprinkle cinnamon streusel mix over butter
  5. Bake 40-45 mins. Cool 10 mins. Drizzle with icing and enjoy!
