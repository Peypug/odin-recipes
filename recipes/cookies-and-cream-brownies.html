Cookies and Cream Brownies

About

Cookies and cream brownies are chocolate brownies topped with creamy goodness and crushed cookies.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar: 5/2 cups
  • Flour: 3/4 cups
  • Cocoa Powder: 1/2 cups
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoons
  • Baking Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Melted Butter: 3/4 cups
  • Eggs: 3
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Chocolate Cookies: 32
  • Whipped Topping: 8 ounces

Baking process

  1. Preheat oven 350 F, grease 9 in baking pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, cocoa, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Whisk butter, eggs, and vanilla extract (seperate from previous step).
  4. Add two bowls together
  5. Pour half batter into baking pan, add 16 cookies on top, add remaining batter.
  6. 30-35 in oven
  7. Let cool 30 mins
  8. Crush remaining cookies and add to whipping mix, spread over brownies
  9. Refrigerate until satisfied!
