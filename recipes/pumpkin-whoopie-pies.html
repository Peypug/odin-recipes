About

A pumpkin whoopie pie is basically a magical sanwich but pumpkin cookies instead of bread and whipped cream instead of sandwich contents.

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar: 2 cups
  • Vegetable Oil: 1 cup
  • Pumpkin Puree: 5/2 cups
  • Eggs: 2
  • Flour: 3 cups
  • Cinnamon: 5/2 tablespoons
  • Ginger: 1/2 tablespoon
  • Cloves: 1/2 tablespoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking Soda: 1 teaspoon
  • Vanilla Extract: 2 teaspoon
  • Egg White: 1
  • Milk: 2 tablespoons
  • Confectioners' Sugar: 2 cups
  • Shortening: 3/4 cups

Baking process

  1. Preheat 350 F, grease baking sheet
  2. Make dough with brown sugar, oil, pumpkin, and eggs and mix. Add flour, cinnamon giner, cloves, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and vanilla together in seperate bowl. Mix the two bowls
  3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto the prepared baking sheets.
  4. Bake 10-12 mins. Cool and make filling.
  5. Make filling with egg white, milk, vanilla, and 1 cup Confectioners' sugar. Mix and add shortening and 1 cup Confectioners's sugar. Beat dough.
  6. Aseemble your magical sandwiches and enjoy!
